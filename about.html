About KIDA Alumni

A lifelong network of learning and impact.

KIDA Alumni connects graduates, educators, and partners in a professional community committed to scholarship, mentorship, and service. We strengthen ties between members while supporting the next generation of learners.

Our Motto

We are Better Together

Scholarship

Promote academic excellence through mentorship, research, and peer support.

Leadership

Cultivate ethical and innovative leaders across sectors and communities.

Service

Advance social impact through collaborative alumni initiatives and partnerships.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant alumni community that fosters lasting connections, drives member growth, and supports the school's legacy.

Our Mission

To unite and empower alumni through networking, service, and collaboration that strengthen our community and support the school's growth.
